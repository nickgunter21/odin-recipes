Grilled Cheese Recipe


Ingredients you'll need:

  • bread
  • butter
  • cheese

Recipe:

  1. Place butter in a non-stick pan and heat until butter is melted
  2. Put two slices of bread on the pan with one slice of cheese on top of each piece of bread
  3. Once the cheese has melted, use a spatula to take one of the slices and flip the bread and cheese so that the two slices of cheese come together and make a sandwich
  4. Keep heating the sandwich until the bread is a golden brown color on both sides
  5. Cut into triangles and serve!
